Angular Or React, a decade long battle!

September, 2020

There are a lot of options for the type of technology you should choose to create your web application. Two of the most popular JavaScript frameworks include Angular and React. They are essential for creating the front-end of any website. Both technologies give each other tough competition as they grow with time. This is a grand thing, but can also cause a lot of confusion among developers and vendors. It’s difficult to choose one over the other, especially since there is a lot of debate over the matter. Many app owners question whether they should go with React app development or Angular app development. In this post, we will clarify the confusion and help you get started on your project. In the rest of the paragraphs, we will mention various factors and try to simplify choosing your JavaScript framework. Our aim here is not to define which is the best one but simply to impart knowledge. You can analyze all the information here and use it to select your preferred choice of framework. We will talk about both the technologies, their differences, and their advantages. So Angular or React? Let’s find out. Angular vs React in 2021: Some Facts and Figures Before we begin, let us start by looking at some numbers that are crucial to understanding the market in 2020. It will help you get an idea of what other developers select for the development of their web application. You too can go with their analysis or choose a platform after gathering all the information. According to Jetbrains, 64% of developers use React regularly as compared to Angular at 11% in the year 2020. This makes React the most popular JavaScript framework globally.
